# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a collaborative environment that is respectful, inclusive,
evidence-driven, and safe for contributors of every background and experience
level.

## Expected behavior

- Give specific, constructive technical feedback.
- Challenge claims with reproducible evidence, not personal criticism.
- Credit code, design, documentation, and demo contributions accurately.
- Protect private data, credentials, and unpublished submission material.
- Resolve disagreements around the project objective and acceptance criteria.
- Respect Kaggle competition rules, team limits, deadlines, and one-submission
  requirements.

Harassment, discriminatory language, deliberate sabotage, credential misuse,
plagiarism, or misrepresentation of evaluation results is not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit or remove unacceptable contributions and restrict
participation when necessary. Sensitive reports should be sent privately to the
repository owner rather than posted publicly.
