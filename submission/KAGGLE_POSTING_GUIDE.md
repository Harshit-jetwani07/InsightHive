3. Upload the cover and evidence images to Media Gallery.
4. Attach the public YouTube video.
5. Attach the live product URL and public GitHub URL.
6. Save, preview, and verify formatting.
7. Click **Submit** in the top-right corner.
8. Re-open the project and verify it is submitted—not a draft.

Official deadline: **July 6, 2026 at 11:59 PM Pacific Time**.
