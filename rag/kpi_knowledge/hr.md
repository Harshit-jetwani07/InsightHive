# HR KPI Playbook

- Voluntary attrition: voluntary departures divided by average headcount.
- Time to fill: days from approved requisition to accepted offer.
- Absence rate: absent workdays divided by available workdays.
- Internal mobility: roles filled internally divided by total roles filled.
- Representation: demographic group share by level, function, and hiring stage.
- Engagement: report survey response rate and question-level trends with sample size.

Guardrail: aggregate small groups and avoid individual-level sensitive inferences.
