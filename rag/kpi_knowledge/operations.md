# Operations KPI Playbook

- Cycle time: elapsed time from work start to completion.
- Throughput: completed units per unit of time.
- On-time delivery: deliveries on or before promise date divided by total deliveries.
- Defect rate: defective units divided by inspected units.
- Capacity utilization: actual output divided by practical capacity.
- Forecast error: report MAE or RMSE together with scale and evaluation period.

Guardrail: segment bottlenecks by process stage, facility, product, and shift.
