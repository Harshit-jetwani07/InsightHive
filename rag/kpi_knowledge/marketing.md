# Marketing KPI Playbook

- Conversion rate: desired actions divided by eligible visits or leads.
- Cost per acquisition: campaign spend divided by attributed acquisitions.
- Return on ad spend: attributed revenue divided by ad spend.
- Click-through rate: clicks divided by impressions.
- Lead-to-customer rate: converted customers divided by qualified leads.
- Incrementality: prefer controlled lift over platform-attributed conversions.

Guardrail: state the attribution window and avoid treating correlation as campaign lift.
