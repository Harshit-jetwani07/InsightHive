# SaaS KPI Playbook

- Monthly recurring revenue: normalized recurring subscription revenue.
- Net revenue retention: starting recurring revenue plus expansion minus contraction and churn.
- Logo churn: customers lost divided by customers at the start of the period.
- Customer acquisition cost: sales and marketing spend divided by new customers.
- Lifetime value: use gross-margin-adjusted revenue and a documented retention assumption.
- Payback period: customer acquisition cost divided by monthly gross profit per customer.

Guardrail: separate recurring revenue from services and one-time implementation fees.
