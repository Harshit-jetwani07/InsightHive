# Retail KPI Playbook

- Revenue growth: compare period-over-period sales using consistent store coverage.
- Gross margin: `(revenue - cost of goods sold) / revenue`; inspect product and region mix.
- Average order value: revenue divided by completed orders.
- Inventory turnover: cost of goods sold divided by average inventory.
- Sell-through rate: units sold divided by units received.
- Return rate: returned units or value divided by completed sales.

Guardrail: seasonality and promotions can distort short comparison windows.
